Introduction

Global NRG Commercial and Advisory Co. Ltd.

Global NRG Co. Ltd. is a joint stock company with natural gas trading activity registered at the Capital Court of Registration under No.: 01 10 04269. The Company is subsidiary of Global NRG Europe Ltd, a European energy trading group. The Company acquired its natural trading licence in 2006.

Global NRG has all the necessary Hungarian licenses from Hungarian Energy Office for trading of natural gas under No.: 245/2009 f Beside its core activity the Company is active in helping and promoting the decrease of risks of the gas consumers, increasing the safety of gas supply, applying effective gas consumption methods, and following up the economic consequences due the possible changes in legal background. The Company grants advisory service to optimise consumers' capacities.

Fields of activities in natural gas market of Global NRG :

    • The Company supplies natural gas to non-residential end-users based on on commercial contract basis
    • The Company is active player in natural gas trading markets
    • The Company grants solution in storage and flexibility duties
    • The Company participates in optimization of free capacities
